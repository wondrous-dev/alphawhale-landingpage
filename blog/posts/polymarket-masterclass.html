Welcome to the Masterclass

This comprehensive guide synthesizes advanced Polymarket trading knowledge into a structured masterclass. Whether you're moving beyond basics or refining existing skills, this serves as your advanced curriculum.

Module 1: Advanced Probability

Calibration mastery:
  • Track all predictions
  • Measure calibration over time
  • Systematically improve
Conditional probability:
  • If A happens, what's P(B)?
  • Bayesian reasoning
  • Sequential updating
Probability distributions:
  • Not just point estimates
  • Ranges and confidence intervals
  • Fat tail awareness
Exercise: Track 50 predictions with probabilities. Measure calibration at different confidence levels.
