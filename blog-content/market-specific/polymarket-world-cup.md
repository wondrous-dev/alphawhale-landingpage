## Common Mistakes

Avoid these World Cup trading errors:

**Recency bias:** Overweighting most recent matches.

**Club form extrapolation:** Club success doesn't equal national team success.

**Historical determinism:** Past success doesn't guarantee future results.

**Draw overreaction:** Group difficulty assessments often wrong.

**Star player focus:** Teams matter more than individuals.

## Unique World Cup Dynamics
