## Getting Started with Deposits

Before you can trade on Polymarket, you need to fund your account. Polymarket uses USDC (a stablecoin worth $1 USD) for all trading. This guide walks you through every deposit method available.

Understanding your options helps you choose the fastest and cheapest way to get started.

## What You Need Before Depositing

Prerequisites for funding:

**Connected wallet:** You need a crypto wallet connected to Polymarket.

**Identity verification:** Some deposit methods require KYC verification.

**Payment method:** Credit card, bank account, or existing crypto.

**Polymarket account:** Active account on the platform.

## Deposit Methods Overview

Available funding options:

**Credit/debit card:** Fastest for beginners, higher fees.

**Bank transfer:** Lower fees, slower processing.

**Crypto transfer:** Send USDC or ETH directly.

**Third-party onramps:** Services like MoonPay, Transak.

**Existing crypto:** Convert crypto you already own.

## Depositing with Credit Card

Step-by-step credit card deposit:

**Step 1:** Log into Polymarket and click "Deposit."

**Step 2:** Select "Card" as your payment method.

**Step 3:** Enter the amount you want to deposit.

**Step 4:** Complete identity verification if required.

**Step 5:** Enter your card details.

**Step 6:** Confirm the transaction.

**Processing time:** Usually instant to a few minutes.

**Fees:** Typically 2-5% depending on provider.

## Depositing with Bank Transfer

Step-by-step bank deposit:

**Step 1:** Click "Deposit" on Polymarket.

**Step 2:** Select bank transfer option.

**Step 3:** Complete verification if required.

**Step 4:** Follow instructions to link your bank.

**Step 5:** Initiate the transfer.

**Step 6:** Wait for funds to arrive.

**Processing time:** 1-5 business days.

**Fees:** Usually lower than credit card, sometimes free.

## Depositing with Crypto

If you already have crypto:

**Step 1:** Get your Polymarket deposit address.

**Step 2:** Note the network (usually Polygon).

**Step 3:** Send USDC to your deposit address.

**Step 4:** Wait for confirmation.

**Step 5:** Funds appear in your account.

**Processing time:** Usually minutes.

**Fees:** Network gas fees only.

## Understanding USDC

What is USDC:

**Stablecoin:** USDC is pegged 1:1 to the US dollar.

**Why Polymarket uses it:** Provides stable value for trading.

**Where to get USDC:** Exchanges like Coinbase, Kraken, or through onramps.

**Networks:** USDC exists on multiple networks; Polymarket uses Polygon.

**Converting:** Can convert other crypto to USDC on exchanges.

## Network Considerations

Important network details:

**Polygon network:** Polymarket primarily uses Polygon for lower fees.

**Wrong network risk:** Sending to wrong network can lose funds.

**Double-check:** Always verify network before sending.

**Gas fees:** Polygon has low gas fees compared to Ethereum mainnet.

**Bridge options:** Can bridge from Ethereum if needed.

## Minimum Deposits

Deposit limits:

**Minimum amount:** Usually $1-10 depending on method.

**Maximum amount:** Varies by verification level and method.

**Daily limits:** Some methods have daily limits.

**Verification levels:** Higher limits with more verification.

**Check current limits:** Limits may change, verify on platform.

## Deposit Fees Comparison

Understanding costs:

**Credit card:** 2-5% fee typical.

**Bank transfer:** 0-1% fee typical.

**Crypto transfer:** Network gas fees only (cents on Polygon).

**Third-party providers:** Varies by provider.

**Total cost:** Factor fees into trading calculations.

## Troubleshooting Deposits

Common issues and solutions:

**Card declined:** Try different card or contact bank.

**Verification issues:** Ensure documents are clear and valid.

**Delayed transfer:** Bank transfers can take several days.

**Missing crypto:** Verify correct network and address.

**Amount mismatch:** Check for fees deducted during transfer.

## Security Best Practices

Protecting your deposits:

**Verify addresses:** Double-check all addresses before sending.

**Start small:** Test with small amount first.

**Official channels:** Only use official Polymarket deposit methods.

**Secure connection:** Use secure network when depositing.

**Save records:** Keep records of all deposits.

## Verification Requirements

KYC considerations:

**Why verification:** Required for fiat deposits and higher limits.

**What's needed:** Usually ID and proof of address.

**Processing time:** Usually minutes to hours.

**Privacy:** Information protected by privacy policies.

**Benefits:** Higher limits and more deposit options.

When to deposit:

**Business hours:** Bank transfers process during business hours.

**Weekend deposits:** May not process until Monday.
