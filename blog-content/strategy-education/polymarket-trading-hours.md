**News cycles:** News breaks at predictable times.

**Event timing:** Events occur at specific times.

## Peak Trading Hours

When markets are most active:

**US business hours:** 9 AM - 5 PM Eastern Time typically busiest.

**Overlap periods:** When multiple regions are active.

**Event times:** Activity spikes around major events.

**News releases:** High activity after major news.

**Market opens:** When related traditional markets open.

## Benefits of Peak Hours

Advantages of busy times:

**Tighter spreads:** More competition among traders.

**Better liquidity:** Easier to enter and exit.

**Faster execution:** Orders fill more quickly.

**Price discovery:** Prices more efficient with more participants.

**Information flow:** More news and information available.

## Off-Peak Trading

Trading during quiet periods:

**Lower liquidity:** May be harder to fill large orders.

**Price gaps:** Prices may gap between trades.

**Opportunities:** Some opportunities in thin markets.

**Patience required:** May need to wait for fills.
