## Polymarket Review Overview

Polymarket is the world's leading prediction market platform. This comprehensive review examines its features, user experience, strengths, weaknesses, and who it's best suited for.

## Platform Overview

**What it is:** A prediction market where users trade on the outcomes of future events.

**How it works:** Buy and sell shares priced between $0-$1 reflecting probability. Winning shares pay $1.

**Founded:** 2020

**Blockchain:** Polygon (Ethereum layer 2)

**Currency:** USDC stablecoin

Polymarket offers the broadest selection of prediction markets:
